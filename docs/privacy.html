Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Summary

Beautiful Quran does not collect, store, or share any personal data. There are no accounts, no analytics, no crash reporting, and no advertising SDKs. The app is a standalone reader that works fully offline after initial setup.

What information we collect

None. The app does not collect any personal information, device identifiers, usage statistics, or any other data. All preferences (reciter choice, theme, font size, reading position) are stored locally on your device in standard Android SharedPreferences and are never transmitted anywhere.

Internet access

The app requests internet access only to stream recitation audio files from everyayah.com, a public service that hosts Quran recitations. Standard HTTP requests are made to fetch MP3 files. No identifying information is sent with these requests beyond what your device and network normally include in any HTTP request (e.g., IP address, User-Agent string).

Audio files are cached locally so recently heard surahs work offline. This cache is stored in the app's private files directory and is never transmitted or shared.

Third-party services

The app connects to one external service:

  • everyayah.com — hosts public-domain recitation audio. The app fetches MP3 files from this domain. No other third-party services, SDKs, or APIs are used.

The app uses no advertising, analytics, crash reporting, or push notification services.

Data storage

All app data is stored locally on your device:

  • Quran text database — shipped with the app in the APK, extracted to the app's noBackupFilesDir
  • Audio cache — stored in filesDir/audio, excluded from system backups
  • User preferences — stored in SharedPreferences

Children's privacy

The app does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. All content is religious text appropriate for all ages.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes in the future, the updated date at the top of this page will be revised. The app itself will not change its data practices without a corresponding update to this policy.

Contact

This app is open source. For questions about this policy, please open an issue on the GitHub repository.
