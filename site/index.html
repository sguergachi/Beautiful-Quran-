How it works

Open a surah and tap play. As the reciter recites, each Arabic word fades in from the ink of the page — timed to the millisecond. The English gloss rises beneath it. Skip, repeat, or narrow playback to any ayah range. The paper stays quiet; only the words move.

Reading modes

Arabic & English — Arabic flows right-to-left with the English gloss under each word; the flowing translation below.

English — the gloss becomes the primary line, lighting word-by-word on the same timings.

Optional transliteration. Optional ayah translation. All of it serif, all of it paper.
